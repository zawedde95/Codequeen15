About REA HOTEL

Discover our story and commitment to hospitality

Welcome to REA HOTEL

Founded in 2015, REA HOTEL has grown to become one of Nansana's most beloved accommodations. Our mission is simple: provide a clean, safe, and friendly environment where guests can relax and feel at home.

We are family‑owned and operated, and we take pride in our personal touch. Whether you need help arranging transportation, recommendations for local attractions, or a special meal prepared, our staff is always ready to assist.

Conveniently located just 20 minutes from Kampala city centre, we offer easy access to business districts, shopping, and entertainment – while still providing a quiet retreat from the hustle and bustle.
