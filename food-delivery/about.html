About QuickBite

Bringing delicious meals to your door

Our Story

QuickBite was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: connect hungry customers with the best local restaurants and deliver fresh, tasty food fast. We started as a small team of food lovers in Kampala and have grown to partner with over 50 restaurants across the city.

We believe that great food should be accessible to everyone. That's why we focus on quality, speed, and friendly service. Whether you're craving pizza, sushi, or a hearty burger, QuickBite brings it to you.
