About Modern Living

Crafting timeless pieces since 2015

Our Story

Modern Living was born from a passion for clean design and quality craftsmanship. We started as a small workshop in Kampala, creating custom furniture for friends and family. Today, we're proud to offer a curated collection of sofas, tables, beds, and accessories – all made with sustainable materials and a love for detail.

Every piece is designed to bring comfort and beauty into your home. We work directly with local artisans to ensure fair wages and traditional techniques are preserved.

Sustainability

We use FSC‑certified wood and low‑VOC finishes.

Handcrafted

Each item is made by hand, not mass‑produced.
